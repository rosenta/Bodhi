# Aristotle First Principles Deconstruction

## The Irreducible Move

> Don't build anything yet. Spend 30 days with 30 carefully selected verses.
> Record raw, unscripted 60-second reactions. Post them. See what happens.
> THEN build the website. THEN build the brand. THEN scale.

## 10 Irreducible Truths

1. These texts contain transformative ideas (survived 1,200+ years because they work)
2. Transformation is experiential, not informational
3. People don't want more content — they want change
4. One verse deeply understood > 580 verses skimmed
5. The Guru-Disciple structure IS the product
6. Attention is the scarcest resource
7. Sanskrit has aesthetic power independent of meaning
8. The person doing this project IS the first student
9. Distribution is free
10. A single truth-dense piece spreads further than 100 polished-but-generic posts

## Assumptions Killed

| Old Assumption | New Truth |
|---|---|
| Digitize all 775 verses first | Start with 30 most potent ones |
| Build full website first | Build a transformation EXPERIENCE |
| Need professional production | Truth-density + authenticity > polish |
| Multi-platform from day 1 | One platform, one format, 30-day test |
| Commentary = the content | PRACTICE = the content |
| Need a team | Your journey IS the content |
| Brand identity before launch | Ship before you're ready; brand emerges |
| This is a content business | This is a TRANSFORMATION business |

## Three Approaches (Pick One to Start)

### A: The 30-Day Experiment
Pick 30 verses. Record yourself explaining what each means to YOU. Post daily. Gather data.

### B: The Transformation Engine
Build a 40-day guided practice tool (Pancha Kosha framework). One verse + one practice + one journal prompt per day.

### C: The Sanskrit Art Project
Lead with beauty. Display verses as visual art. Let the verse hit people raw. Teaching happens in the community that forms.

## The Decision
We're going with a HYBRID: Curate the 30 most powerful verses for immediate content (Approach A) WHILE building the full corpus for the website (long-term infrastructure).
