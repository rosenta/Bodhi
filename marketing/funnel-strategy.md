# Marketing Funnel Strategy: Project Bodhi

## Funnel Overview

```
  AWARENESS (Top)         ENGAGEMENT (Middle)        CONVERSION (Bottom)
  ==================      ====================       ====================
  How they find us        What keeps them            What they pay for
  ------------------      --------------------       --------------------
  YouTube SEO             Daily Verse email          Online courses
  Google organic          Website reading journey    App subscription
  Instagram Reels         Community (Discord)        Workshops
  TikTok discovery        Podcast subscription       Merchandise
  Collaborations          Newsletter deep-dives      Membership/Patreon
  Word of mouth           Challenge series           Consulting/speaking
  Reddit/Quora            AI chatbot interaction     Sponsorships
```

## Stage 1: AWARENESS (Top of Funnel)

**Goal:** Get discovered by people who are searching for or open to this content.

### Channel Strategy

#### 1A. YouTube SEO (PRIMARY -- highest ROI channel)

**Why:** Spiritual seekers actively search YouTube. Long-tail keywords like "vivekachudamani verse 1 meaning" have near-zero competition. YouTube videos also rank in Google search results.

**Tactics:**
- Publish 2-3 long-form videos/week (8-15 min verse deep-dives)
- Daily YouTube Shorts (repurposed from Reels)
- Optimize titles for search: "[Verse/Sutra Number] + [Key Concept] + Meaning Explained"
- Custom thumbnails with Sanskrit text + clean design
- End screens driving to playlists (sequential verse journeys)
- Pinned comments with links to website verse pages

**Estimated Reach:** 5K-20K views/video within 6 months for long-tail content (based on comparable niche channels)

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target (Month 3) | Target (Month 6) | Target (Month 12) |
|--------|-------------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Subscribers | 2,000 | 10,000 | 40,000 |
| Monthly views | 50,000 | 200,000 | 800,000 |
| Avg. view duration | >50% | >50% | >50% |
| Search impressions | 100,000 | 500,000 | 2,000,000 |
| Click-through rate | >5% | >6% | >7% |

**Tracking Tool:** YouTube Studio Analytics + TubeBuddy for keyword tracking

---

#### 1B. Google Organic / Website SEO (SECONDARY -- long-term moat)

**Why:** "vivekachudamani" gets 8,100+ monthly searches with almost no quality results. A well-structured site can dominate these SERPs within 6-12 months.

**Tactics:**
- Individual pages for each of the 580 verses and 195 sutras (775 indexable pages)
- Pillar content pages for major concepts (Pancha Kosha, 8 Limbs, Maya, Tat Tvam Asi)
- Blog posts targeting educational keywords ("what is advaita vedanta," etc.)
- FAQ schema markup on verse pages
- Internal linking between related verses/concepts

**Estimated Reach:** 20K monthly organic visitors by month 6, 100K+ by month 12

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target (Month 3) | Target (Month 6) | Target (Month 12) |
|--------|-------------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Indexed pages | 200 | 500 | 775+ |
| Monthly organic traffic | 5,000 | 20,000 | 100,000 |
| Keyword rankings (top 10) | 30 keywords | 100 keywords | 300+ keywords |
| Avg. time on site | >3 min | >3.5 min | >4 min |
| Bounce rate | <60% | <55% | <50% |

**Tracking Tools:** Google Search Console, Google Analytics 4, Ahrefs (free webmaster tools)

---

#### 1C. Instagram Reels + TikTok (TERTIARY -- viral potential)

**Why:** Short-form video has the highest discovery potential. Spiritual quote content performs well on Instagram. TikTok's algorithm favors new creators.

**Tactics:**
- Daily quote card posts (verse of the day)
- 3-4 Reels/week (60-second verse explanations)
- 2 carousels/week (deeper breakdowns, swipeable formats)
- Hashtag strategy: mix of broad (#spirituality, #meditation) and niche (#advaita, #vivekachudamani, #yogasutras)
- Repost to TikTok with minor format adjustments
- Instagram Stories: behind-the-scenes, polls, "which verse speaks to you?"

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target (Month 3) | Target (Month 6) | Target (Month 12) |
|--------|-------------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Instagram followers | 2,000 | 8,000 | 30,000 |
| TikTok followers | 1,000 | 5,000 | 20,000 |
| Avg. reel views | 5,000 | 15,000 | 50,000 |
| Engagement rate | >4% | >4% | >3.5% |
| Profile visits/week | 500 | 2,000 | 8,000 |

**Tracking Tools:** Instagram Insights, TikTok Analytics, Publer analytics

---

#### 1E. Word of Mouth & Organic Sharing

**Tactics:**
- Make content inherently shareable (beautiful quote cards, insightful 60-sec videos)
- Add share prompts: "Send this to someone who needs to hear this today"
- Create "challenge" content: "7-Day Self-Inquiry Challenge" that people share with friends
- Encourage verse-by-verse reading groups (study circles)

**KPI:** Track share rate on posts (target: >2% of reach)

## Stage 2: ENGAGEMENT (Middle of Funnel)

**Goal:** Convert casual viewers into committed followers who consume content regularly.

### 2A. Daily Verse Email Newsletter

**Why:** Email is the highest-engagement, algorithm-proof channel. A daily verse email creates a habit loop.

**Mechanics:**
- One verse/sutra per day with: Sanskrit, translation, modern interpretation, reflection prompt
- Automated via n8n + Buttondown/Substack
- Clear CTA on every website page and social bio: "Get one verse of ancient wisdom daily"
- Welcome sequence (5 emails): introduce the project, explain the texts, set expectations

**Conversion Targets:**
| Source | Conversion Rate to Email | Monthly New Subs |
|--------|--------------------------|------------------|
| Website visitors | 3-5% | Month 6: 600-1,000 / Month 12: 3,000-5,000 |
| YouTube description links | 0.5-1% | Month 6: 100-200 / Month 12: 400-800 |
| Instagram bio link | 1-2% | Month 6: 80-160 / Month 12: 300-600 |

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target (Month 3) | Target (Month 6) | Target (Month 12) |
|--------|-------------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Email subscribers | 500 | 2,000 | 8,000 |
| Open rate | >45% | >40% | >38% |
| Click rate | >8% | >7% | >6% |
| Unsubscribe rate | <0.5% | <0.5% | <0.5% |

**Tracking Tool:** Buttondown/Substack analytics

---

### 2B. Website Reading Journey

**Why:** The website is the "home base." A gamified reading journey increases return visits and time-on-site.

**Mechanics:**
- Progress tracker: "You've read 47 of 580 verses" (requires free account)
- Bookmarking and note-taking on verses
- "Continue your journey" email reminders for inactive users
- Related verse recommendations at the bottom of each page
- Weekly/monthly reading plans: "Read Pancha Kosha in 7 days"

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target (Month 6) | Target (Month 12) |
|--------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Registered users | 1,000 | 5,000 |
| Return visitor rate | >30% | >35% |
| Avg. pages per session | >3 | >4 |
| Reading journey completion rate | >5% started, >1% completed | >8% started, >2% completed |

**Tracking Tool:** Google Analytics 4 (events), Supabase user data

---

### 2C. Community (Discord/Telegram)

**Why:** Community creates belonging and retention. Study circles are the traditional format for these texts.

**Mechanics:**
- Free Discord server with channels: #daily-verse, #ask-questions, #study-circle, #introductions
- Weekly live verse reading + discussion (30 min, audio only)
- Monthly themed deep-dives (e.g., "The Pancha Kosha Month")
- Community members help answer questions (reduces creator burden)

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target (Month 6) | Target (Month 12) |
|--------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Community members | 300 | 1,500 |
| Weekly active members | 30% | 25% |
| Messages per week | 200 | 800 |
| Live session attendance | 20-30 | 50-100 |

**Tracking Tool:** Discord analytics, manual tracking

---

### 2D. Podcast Subscription

**Why:** Podcast listeners are highly loyal. Average spiritual podcast listener consumes 4-6 hours/week.

**Mechanics:**
- Weekly 20-30 min episodes: one verse deep-dive + modern application + guided reflection
- Available on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, YouTube (audio-only or minimal visual)
- Cross-promote with email newsletter and YouTube videos

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target (Month 6) | Target (Month 12) |
|--------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Monthly downloads | 2,000 | 10,000 |
| Avg. completion rate | >60% | >65% |
| Subscriber count | 500 | 3,000 |

**Tracking Tool:** Spotify for Podcasters analytics

---

### 2E. Challenge/Series Content

**Why:** Sequential, numbered content creates commitment and FOMO. It gives casual followers a reason to become regular consumers.

**Series to Launch:**
1. **"30 Verses That Will Change How You See Yourself"** (Month 1-2, Instagram/YouTube)
2. **"195 Sutras, 195 Days"** (Month 3+, daily content pillar)
3. **"5-Day Pancha Kosha Challenge"** (Month 4, viral Instagram series)
4. **"The Disciple's Journey"** (Month 6+, animated episodic series)

**KPI:** Series completion rate >15% (viewers who watch episode 1 also watch the final episode)

## Stage 3: CONVERSION (Bottom of Funnel)

**Goal:** Generate revenue from the most engaged segment of the audience.

### 3A. Online Courses

**Product 1: "Vivekachudamani Immersion" (12-week cohort course)**
- Launch: Month 6-8
- Price: $149-299
- Format: Weekly 60-min live session + pre-recorded lectures + community + journal prompts
- Target: 30-50 students per cohort (2 cohorts/year initially)
- Estimated Revenue: $4,500-15,000 per cohort

**Product 2: "Yoga Sutras Self-Paced Course"**
- Launch: Month 9-12
- Price: $79-149
- Format: 4 modules (one per Pada), pre-recorded, evergreen
- Target: 20-50 sales/month once established
- Estimated Revenue: $1,600-7,500/month at scale

**Conversion Funnel:**
```
Email subscriber (8,000) → Course waitlist signup (5% = 400) → Course purchase (15% = 60) → $149 = $8,940/cohort
```

**Tracking Tool:** Teachable/Podia analytics, Stripe

---

### 3B. App Subscription (Future -- Month 12+)

- Daily verse + audio + journal + progress tracking
- Price: $4.99/month or $39.99/year
- Target: 2-5% of email list converts
- At 8,000 email subs, 2% = 160 subscribers = $800/month

**Tracking Tool:** App analytics (RevenueCat), Stripe

---

### 3C. Membership / Patreon

- Launch: Month 4-6
- Tiers: $5/month (early access), $15/month (community access + monthly Q&A), $50/month (1-on-1 guidance)
- Target: 50-200 members by Month 12
- Estimated Revenue: $500-3,000/month

**Conversion Funnel:**
```
YouTube subscriber (40,000) → Patreon page visit (1% = 400) → Conversion (10% = 40) → Avg $12/mo = $480/month
Email subscriber (8,000) → Patreon CTA click (3% = 240) → Conversion (15% = 36) → Avg $15/mo = $540/month
```

**Tracking Tool:** Patreon analytics

---

### 3D. Merchandise

- Launch: Month 6
- Products: Sanskrit calligraphy prints ($25-50), verse journals ($20-30), quote posters ($15-25)
- Print-on-demand (no inventory risk): Printful or Gelato
- Target: 30-80 orders/month by Month 12
- Estimated Revenue: $600-2,400/month (at ~$20 avg profit per order)

**Tracking Tool:** Shopify/Gumroad analytics

---

### 3E. Workshops & Live Events

- Launch: Month 6
- Format: 2-hour online workshops on specific themes ("Understanding Maya," "The 8 Limbs in Daily Life")
- Price: $25-49
- Frequency: 1-2 per month
- Target: 30-60 attendees per workshop
- Estimated Revenue: $750-2,940/month

**Tracking Tool:** Zoom registration + Stripe

---

### 3F. YouTube Ad Revenue

- Available once hitting 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours
- Expected CPM for spiritual content: $3-8
- At 800,000 monthly views (Month 12): $2,400-6,400/month
- Long-form content with 8+ min runtime maximizes mid-roll ad revenue

**Tracking Tool:** YouTube Studio revenue reports

---

### 3G. Sponsorships

- Available at 50K+ subscribers (Month 9-12)
- Target sponsors: meditation apps (Insight Timer, Waking Up), yoga brands, retreat centers, spiritual book publishers
- Rate: $1,000-3,000 per sponsored video at 50K-100K subs
- Frequency: 1-2 per month
- Estimated Revenue: $1,000-6,000/month

## Attribution Model

**How to know what is working:**

1. **UTM parameters on every link:** `?utm_source=youtube&utm_medium=description&utm_campaign=verse42`
2. **Link-in-bio tool** with click tracking (Publer or Linktree)
3. **Welcome email survey:** "How did you find us?" (simple dropdown)
4. **Google Analytics referral reports:** Track which platforms drive website traffic
5. **Course/purchase surveys:** "Where did you first hear about us?"

**Attribution Priority:**
```
First touch:  How did they discover us? → Informs awareness spending
Last touch:   What made them convert? → Informs conversion optimization
Multi-touch:  Full journey → Informs content strategy
```
