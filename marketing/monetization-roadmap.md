# Monetization Roadmap: Project Bodhi

## Guiding Principles

1. **Free first, paid later.** Build trust and audience before asking for money.
2. **The free product must be genuinely excellent.** Paid offerings are premium depth, not paywalled basics.
3. **Conservative estimates throughout.** Every projection uses lower-bound conversion rates.
4. **Revenue follows value.** Every dollar earned should feel earned to the audience.
5. **Costs stay lean.** The AI-automated stack at ~$101/month is the foundation.

## Risk-Adjusted Revenue Scenarios

### What If YouTube Growth Is Slower?

If YouTube reaches only 15,000 subs by Month 12 (instead of 40,000):
- YouTube ad revenue drops to ~$1,500/month (from $5,000)
- Sponsorships drop to ~$500/month (from $3,000)
- Course enrollment drops ~30% (less funnel volume)
- **Adjusted Month 12 revenue: ~$6,500/month** (still profitable)
- **Mitigation:** Double down on SEO (website traffic) and email list as conversion channels

### What If No One Buys the Course?

If the first course cohort gets only 10 students (instead of 40):
- Revenue impact: -$6,000 per cohort
- **Mitigation:** Lower price to $99, offer beta pricing, gather testimonials, relaunch
- The course is not critical to profitability until Phase 2. YouTube + Patreon + workshops cover costs well before then.

### What If a Platform Changes Its Algorithm?

- **YouTube:** Already diversified with website SEO + email list. YouTube is search-driven for this niche, not algorithm-driven, so less vulnerable.
- **Instagram:** Loss of Instagram would reduce awareness but not revenue. Instagram drives ~10% of email signups.
- **Mitigation:** The email list is the most important asset. Grow it aggressively from Month 1.

| Gate | Timing | Metric | Go | No-Go Action |

## Summary: The Path to $25K/Month

```
Month 1-3:   $0/month     → Build content, build audience, build trust
Month 4-6:   $500-2,300   → YouTube ads + Patreon + first workshop
Month 7-9:   $3,200-6,500 → + first course cohort + sponsorships + merch
Month 10-12: $9,200-19,700→ + second course + self-paced course + scaling sponsors
Month 18:    $15,000-20,000→ + app subscription + corporate
Month 24:    $25,000-30,000→ Mature, diversified, sustainable

Total Year 1 Revenue (conservative): $68,000
Total Year 1 Costs: ~$5,000
Total Year 1 Net Profit: ~$63,000

Total Year 2 Revenue (projected): $320,000
Total Year 2 Costs: ~$36,000
Total Year 2 Net Profit: ~$284,000
```

The economics work because:
1. AI automation keeps content production costs near zero
2. Digital products have near-zero marginal cost
3. 775 verses provide 2+ years of content without creative burnout
4. Evergreen content compounds (a video made in Month 1 still generates views and revenue in Month 24)
5. The audience is underserved and willing to pay for quality
