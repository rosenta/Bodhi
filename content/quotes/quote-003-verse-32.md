**Sanskrit**: स्वस्वरूपानुसन्धानं भक्तिरित्यभिधीयते

**Translation**: "The continuous inquiry into your own true nature — that is the highest devotion."

**Caption**: Forget candles. Forget chanting. Forget kneeling. The most sacred act you can perform is asking "Who am I?" and refusing to accept a borrowed answer. That's what Shankaracharya called devotion. Not worship of something out there — but relentless curiosity about what's in here.
