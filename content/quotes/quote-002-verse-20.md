**Sanskrit**: ब्रह्म सत्यं जगन्मिथ्या

**Translation**: "Consciousness alone is real. The world is appearance."

**Caption**: Everything you're chasing — the job, the relationship, the follower count — is the movie. You are the screen. The screen doesn't care what movie is playing. It remains unchanged. That's the most liberating thing Shankaracharya ever said.
