**Sanskrit**: विलीयमानेऽपि वपुष्यमुष्मिन् न लीयते कुम्भ इवाम्बरं स्वयम्

**Translation**: "When the pot breaks, the sky inside doesn't shatter. Neither will you."

**Caption**: Your body will age. Your identity will shift. Your heart will one day stop. But the awareness reading these words has never been born and will never die. You are the sky, not the pot. Shankaracharya said this with such certainty because he wasn't guessing. He was pointing at something you can verify right now — close your eyes and look.
