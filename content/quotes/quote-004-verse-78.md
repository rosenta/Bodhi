**Sanskrit**: शब्दादिभिः पञ्चभिरेव पञ्च पञ्चत्वमापुः स्वगुणेन बद्धाः

**Translation**: "Five senses chasing five objects — a moth drawn to five flames at once."

**Caption**: A moth dies chasing one light. You're chasing five — on a screen designed to kill your attention. Sound. Sight. Touch. Taste. Smell. Each one a hook. Shankaracharya saw this 1,200 years before smartphones. The solution isn't to destroy the senses. It's to stop being dragged by them.
