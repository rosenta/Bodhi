**Sanskrit**: जन्तूनां नरजन्म दुर्लभमतः पुंस्त्वं ततो विप्रता

**Translation**: "Among all living beings, human birth is the rarest. Rarer still is the desire to know who you truly are."

**Caption**: You won the cosmic lottery just by being here. 1 in 400 trillion odds. And now you have the one thing no other creature has — the ability to ask "Who am I?" Most people never use it. Will you?
