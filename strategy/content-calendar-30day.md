# 30-Day Launch Content Calendar -- Instagram

> Strategy: Start with the most relatable, emotionally resonant verses. Build curiosity in Week 1, deepen understanding in Week 2, challenge assumptions in Week 3, and invite transformation in Week 4.

---

## Posting Schedule

**Reels**: Mon, Wed, Fri, Sun -- 6:00 AM IST / 7:30 PM EST (previous evening)
**Carousels**: Tue, Sat -- 12:00 PM IST / 8:00 PM IST
**Quote Cards**: Thu -- 6:00 AM IST
**Stories**: Daily -- 9:00 AM IST (engagement), 6:00 PM IST (behind-the-scenes)

Rationale: Spiritual content audiences peak at early morning (sadhana time) and late evening (reflection time). 6 AM IST catches India's morning seekers AND US evening scrollers. Sunday is the highest-engagement day for contemplative content.
