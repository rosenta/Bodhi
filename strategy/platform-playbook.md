## 1. INSTAGRAM (Primary Platform -- Months 1-3)

### Content Mix

| Format | Share | Frequency | Purpose |
|--------|-------|-----------|---------|
| Reels | 40% | 4x/week (Mon, Wed, Fri, Sun) | Discovery + viral reach |
| Carousels | 30% | 2x/week (Tue, Sat) | Save-worthy education + depth |
| Quote Cards | 20% | 1x/week (Thu) | Shareability + aesthetic brand |
| Stories | 10% | Daily, 2-4 frames | Engagement + community |

### Posting Times (IST / EST)

| Day | Best Time (IST) | Why |
|-----|-----------------|-----|
| Mon-Fri | 6:00 AM | India morning sadhana crowd + US previous-evening scrollers (7:30 PM EST) |
| Sat-Sun | 8:00 AM | Weekend seekers wake later; higher leisure-scroll time |
| Stories | 9:00 AM + 6:00 PM | Bookend the day: morning intention, evening reflection |

Second-best slot: 12:00 PM IST for carousels (lunch-break learning).

- `#philosophy` (12M)

**Tier 2 -- Niche Vedanta (5-6 per post)**
This is where you compete and win. 10K-500K posts. Higher engagement rates, more targeted audience.
- `#vedanta` (450K)
- `#advaita` (180K)
- `#vivekachudamani` (5K -- you can OWN this tag)
- `#shankaracharya` (30K)
- `#yogasutras` (200K)
- `#patanjali` (120K)
- `#hinduphilosophy` (150K)

**Tier 3 -- Branded/Series (2-3 per post)**
Create and own these. Use them consistently so followers can browse your content by series.
- `#projectbodhi` (branded -- create from Day 1)
- `#30daysofviveka` (series tag for the 30-day launch)
- `#panchakosha` (series tag for the 5-sheaths content)

**Hashtag rules**:
- Use 12-15 total per post (current Instagram sweet spot; more than 20 gets suppressed)
- Rotate Tier 1 tags between posts to avoid being flagged as spammy
- ALWAYS include `#vivekachudamani` -- you will own this tag within 30 days
- Place hashtags in the CAPTION, not comments (algorithm weights caption hashtags higher since 2024)

### Engagement Tactics (Do These Daily, Non-Negotiable)

**Pre-posting ritual (15 min/day)**:
1. Comment thoughtfully on 5 posts from accounts with 10K-100K followers in the Vedanta/yoga/meditation niche. Not "nice post" -- write 2-3 sentences that add genuine value. Examples:
   - On a Sarvapriyananda clip: "This connects beautifully to Vivekachudamani verse 20 where Shankara draws the exact same distinction between the changing and the changeless."
   - On a meditation teacher's post: "The Yoga Sutras actually classify this as Vikalpa -- the third vritti. Imagination that feels real but hasn't happened. Naming it takes away half its power."

2. Reply to EVERY comment on your own posts within 2 hours of posting. Ask a follow-up question in your reply to keep the thread going.

3. DM anyone who shares your content to their stories. Template: "Thank you for sharing this. Which part landed hardest for you? I am curious."

**Engagement-optimized post structures**:
- End every caption with a specific question (not "thoughts?" but "Which of these 5 is running YOUR life right now? Drop a number.")
- Use "Save this for later" as a CTA on educational content (saves are weighted 3x in the algorithm)
- Use "Send this to someone who needs it" on emotional content (shares are the #1 algorithm signal)

### Collaboration Strategy

**Month 1**: Comment and engage. Do not pitch yet. Build genuine relationships.

**Month 2**: Reach out to these tiers:
- **Micro (5K-50K)**: Yoga teachers who post about philosophy, not just asana. Meditation guides. Sanskrit learners. Pitch: "I have been creating verse-by-verse Vivekachudamani content and your audience would love this. Want to do a joint reel where I explain a verse and you lead a 60-second practice based on it?"
- **Mid (50K-500K)**: Vedanta teachers, philosophy channels, mindfulness brands. Pitch: "Collaboration reel or carousel swap. Your teaching + my visual storytelling."

**Month 3**: Pitch to larger accounts (500K+) with data from your first 60 days.

**Collaboration formats that work in this niche**:
- Split-screen reel: you explain the verse, they lead a practice
- Carousel collaboration: you write, they design (or vice versa)
- Instagram Live: joint verse study session (30 minutes, repurpose as clips)
- Story takeover: you take over their stories for a day with a "5 verses in 5 stories" series

### Instagram-Specific Features to Leverage

- **Pinned Posts**: Pin your 3 best-performing reels to the top of your grid. Update monthly.
- **Guides**: Create an Instagram Guide: "Vivekachudamani in 10 Essential Verses" -- curate your best posts into a guided reading order.
- **Reels Remix**: Enable remix on all reels. When someone remixes your content, it drives discovery.
- **Collab Posts**: Use the Collab feature (dual-author posts) for every collaboration. Both audiences see it natively.
- **Broadcast Channel**: Launch a broadcast channel called "Daily Verse" by Week 3. One verse + one sentence daily. This builds a notification-enabled audience.

## 3. TIKTOK (Launch Month 2-3)

### Content Adaptation Strategy

Repurpose Instagram reels with these modifications:
- **Faster pacing**: Cut 3 seconds off the hook. TikTok attention spans are shorter.
- **More text on screen**: TikTok users scroll with sound off more than Instagram users. Every key point needs a text overlay.
- **Stitch/Duet bait**: End reels with provocative statements that invite disagreement. "Yoga has nothing to do with your body. Fight me in the comments."

### Trending Sound Strategy

Do NOT chase random trending sounds. Instead:
- **Use contemplative trending sounds**: Soft piano, ambient, nature sounds that trend periodically. These create the right mood.
- **Use "storytime" trending sounds**: Any trending audio that starts a narrative works for the disciple-guru stories.
- **Original audio**: Use a consistent opening sound (singing bowl + your voice saying "Vivekachudamani verse ___"). When your audio trends, it drives massive discovery.
- **Silence is a strategy**: Some of the most viral spiritual content on TikTok uses deliberate silence as a pattern-interrupt against the noise. Text-on-screen with no audio, then a sudden voiceover.

### Duet/Stitch Strategy

- **Stitch wellness influencers**: Find clips where someone says "yoga changed my life" and stitch with "Patanjali would agree... but probably not for the reason you think." Then explain Sutra 1.2.
- **Duet meditation teachers**: Add commentary to their guided meditations with Vivekachudamani context.
- **Stitch existential crisis content**: When someone posts about feeling lost, stitch with the disciple's confession (Verse 35-40). "2,500 years ago, someone said EXACTLY what you just said."
- **Stitch philosophy content**: React to Western philosophy clips with Vedanta parallels. "Descartes said 'I think therefore I am.' Shankara said 'Who is the I that thinks?'"

### TikTok-Specific Tactics

- **Post 2-3x/day** for the first 30 days (TikTok rewards volume in the early growth phase)
- **Go Live at 1,000 followers**: TikTok Live is the fastest community-building tool on the platform. Do "Ask me about any verse" sessions.
- **Use TikTok Series** (paid content feature) for a structured "Vivekachudamani 101" course once you hit 10K.
- **Creator Fund**: Opt in immediately. Spiritual content gets decent CPM on TikTok ($0.02-0.05/view).

**Daily X routine (10 min)**:

## 5. NEWSLETTER (Launch Week 2)

### Platform

Use Substack or Beehiiv. Recommendation: **Beehiiv** for better growth tools (referral program, recommendation network, A/B testing subject lines). Free tier is sufficient for first 2,500 subscribers.

### Newsletter Name

"The Daily Verse" or "The Crest-Jewel" (weekly, not daily -- despite the name, "daily" creates urgency in the brand name).

### Format (Weekly Email)

Send every Sunday at 7:00 AM IST / Saturday 8:30 PM EST.

**Template**:

> **Subject line**: [see subject line bank below]
>
> **Section 1 -- The Verse** (30 seconds to read)
> Sanskrit (Devanagari) + transliteration + English translation. Beautiful formatting.
>
> **Section 2 -- What This Means For Your Life** (90 seconds to read)
> 3-4 paragraph modern interpretation. Conversational. Personal. Reference the week's Instagram content.
>
> **Section 3 -- The Practice** (30 seconds to read)
> One specific, timed practice for the week. "This week, try this for 5 minutes a day."
>
> **Section 4 -- From the Community** (30 seconds to read)
> Highlight one comment, DM, or response from the week's social content. Shows community is alive.
>
> **Section 5 -- This Week's Content** (15 seconds to scan)
> Links to the week's best reel, carousel, and quote card.

### Subject Lines That Get Opened

These are designed for 40%+ open rates in the spiritual niche (which averages 25-35%):

1. "You beat 400 trillion odds to be here. Now what?"
2. "The question that woke up a 2,500-year-old student"
3. "There are 5 layers hiding the real you"
4. "A snake that was never real (and your version of it)"
5. "Three words. One identity crisis. Zero distance to travel."
6. "Your mind built the prison. Your mind holds the key."
7. "The moth, the deer, and your notification screen"
8. "Nobody can free your soul (here is why that is good news)"
9. "The duckweed is already covering your clarity"
10. "What remains when you stop doing everything?"
11. "Poison dressed as pleasure (and the real nectar)"
12. "When the pot breaks, the sky does not shatter"

**Subject line rules**:
- Never exceed 50 characters (mobile preview cuts off at ~50)
- Use intrigue, not clickbait. The verse content IS inherently intriguing.
- Lowercase feels more intimate and less promotional
- No emojis in subject lines (this audience responds to gravitas, not playfulness)

### Lead Magnet

**"The 10 Verses That Change Everything"** -- A beautifully designed PDF (10 pages, one verse per page) with:
- Sanskrit calligraphy
- English translation
- Modern interpretation
- Practice prompt

Gate it behind email signup. Promote on Instagram bio link, story swipe-ups, and at the end of every reel.

Estimated conversion: Instagram bio link click-through of 3-5%, email conversion of 30-40% on a well-designed landing page = ~150 subscribers per 10,000 Instagram followers.

### Newsletter Growth Tactics

- Add "Join 500+ seekers getting one verse every Sunday" to your Instagram bio
- Mention the newsletter at the end of 1 reel per week ("I go deeper on this in my weekly email -- link in bio")
- Use Beehiiv's recommendation network to cross-promote with other philosophy/spirituality newsletters
- Offer the lead magnet as a "thank you" for sharing your posts to stories
